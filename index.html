Short Bio

My name is Jiawei Yu. Currently, an algorithm engineer in speech processing at Intengine Technology Corporation. Before that, I obtained M.S. degree from the Department of Information Science at Beijing Language and Culture University, advised by Prof. Jinsong Zhang. My current research fields include language recognition using articulatory features, multilingual speech recognition and speaker recognition using speaker embeddings.
